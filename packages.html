FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I mix packages? +
Absolutely. You can combine any services based on your needs. For example, take design and materials from us but arrange your own workers. We're flexible — just tell us what you need.
How long does a typical project take? +
It depends on the scope. A single room (like a bedroom wardrobe) typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full home interior can take 4–8 weeks. We give you a clear timeline at the start of every project.
Do you work outside Surat? +
Our primary service area is Surat and nearby areas. For material supply, we can arrange delivery to a wider radius. Contact us with your location and we'll let you know what's possible.
How do I get a quote? +
Simply WhatsApp us or fill out our contact form. Share your requirements and we'll provide a detailed, no-obligation quote within 24 hours.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfer. We typically work with a 50% advance to begin and 50% on completion.
