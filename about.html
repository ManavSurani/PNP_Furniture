Our Story
& Mission

Who We Are

Built on Passion
& Precision

PnP Interior Consultant was born from a simple belief — that every person deserves a beautiful, functional living and working space, regardless of budget.

Founded by Nisarg and Pratik Surani, we combine creative design sensibility with hands-on expertise to deliver end-to-end furniture and interior solutions across Surat and beyond.

Work With Us →
2014
The Beginning

Nisarg and Pratik started PnP from a small hardware shop in Surat with a vision to make quality interior solutions accessible to all.

2016
Growing the Team

Expanded with a team of skilled carpenters and designers to take on residential and commercial interior projects across the city.

2019
New Showroom

Moved into our dedicated showroom at Hilton Business Center, allowing clients to experience furniture quality and finishes firsthand.

2022
500+ Projects Milestone

Crossed 500 completed projects — from modular kitchens to complete office interiors — earning a trusted reputation in Surat.

Today
Full-Service Excellence

Offering complete interior solutions: design, materials, skilled labor, or the complete package — always at the best value.

The Founders

Meet Nisarg & Pratik

NS

Nisarg Surani

Co-Founder & Design Lead

Nisarg brings a deep passion for beautiful, livable interiors. With an instinctive eye for proportion, material, and light, he transforms client briefs into spaces that feel both aspirational and deeply personal.

His approach centers on listening first — understanding how you live, work, and want to feel in your space before a single design decision is made.

PS

Pratik Surani

Co-Founder & Operations Head

Pratik is the engine that makes every project run smoothly. He manages material procurement, craftsman coordination, and on-site execution with an obsessive attention to timelines and quality standards.

His relationships with material suppliers and his team of skilled craftsmen mean that PnP always delivers exactly what was promised — on time and within budget.
